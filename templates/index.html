Greenlight was made to give back to the 42 community.


The plants were selected with purpose; the hydroponic tower contains edible plants for the local cantina, and air purifying plants for the lab. Parts of the structure was made through 3D modeling and printing as well as custom programmed microcontrollers.
Project Greenlight

Let there be light!

Greenlight uses LED grow lights, different spectrum combinations to faciliate different stages of growth; blue promotes leaf growth while red induces flowering. The temperature and humidity levels are detected via an Arduino and a RaspberryPi, then sent to our site.
